Weekly wellness snapshot

A balanced outline to keep your meals and movement flowing with ease.

Monday

  • Sunrise: Herbal tea and journaling check-in
  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt parfait with berries
  • Lunch: Quinoa bowl with roasted veggies
  • Evening: Sunset stroll with mindful breathing

Wednesday

  • Sunrise: Stretch series and gratitude list
  • Breakfast: Veggie omelet with whole-grain toast
  • Lunch: Lentil soup and citrus salad
  • Evening: Yoga flow and digital sunset

Friday

  • Sunrise: Hydration check and intention setting
  • Breakfast: Overnight oats with chia seeds
  • Lunch: Hummus wrap with crunchy veggies
