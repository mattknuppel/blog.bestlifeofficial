---
title: "Sheet Pan Citrus Salmon"
description: "One-pan salmon with broccolini and sweet potatoes drizzled in a zesty citrus glaze."
coverImage: "/images/recipe-sheet-pan-salmon.svg"
servings: 4
prepTime: 15
cookTime: 20
macros:
  kcal: 480
  protein: 35
  carbs: 32
  fat: 22
ingredients:
  - 1 1/2 pounds salmon fillets
  - 2 medium sweet potatoes, cubed
  - 2 bunches broccolini, trimmed
  - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  - Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  - 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  - 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  - 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  - Sea salt and black pepper
steps:
  - Heat oven to 425°F (220°C). Toss sweet potatoes with 1 tablespoon olive oil, paprika, salt, and pepper; roast for 10 minutes.
  - Add broccolini to the pan, drizzle with remaining olive oil, and roast 5 minutes.
  - Whisk lemon zest, lemon juice, mustard, maple syrup, garlic, salt, and pepper. Push vegetables aside, add salmon, brush with glaze, and roast 8–10 minutes more until salmon flakes.
  - Serve with extra glaze spooned over the top.
tags:
  - pescatarian
  - weeknight
  - high-protein
---

Sheet pan dinners keep weeknights calm. Swap broccolini for asparagus when it’s in season.
