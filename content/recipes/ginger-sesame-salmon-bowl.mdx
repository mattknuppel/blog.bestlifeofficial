  - "1 tbsp sesame oil"

  - "1 cup cooked brown rice"
  - "1 cup edamame"
  - "1 cup shredded cabbage"
  - "2 tbsp toasted sesame seeds"
instructions:
  - "Whisk tamari, sesame oil, and ginger. Brush over salmon."
  - "Bake salmon at 200°C for 10–12 minutes."
  - "Divide rice, edamame, and cabbage between bowls; top with salmon and sesame seeds."

## Make ahead

Portion rice and vegetables into airtight containers. Add salmon just before eating to preserve texture.
