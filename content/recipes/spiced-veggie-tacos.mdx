---
title: "Spiced Veggie Tacos"
description: "Fast weeknight tacos with black beans, roasted vegetables, and a lime yogurt drizzle."
coverImage: "/images/recipe-veggie-tacos.svg"
servings: 4
prepTime: 15
cookTime: 15
macros:
  kcal: 430
  protein: 19
  carbs: 52
  fat: 16
ingredients:
  - 2 cups cauliflower florets
  - 1 red onion, sliced
  - 1 zucchini, diced
  - 1 tablespoon olive oil
  - 2 teaspoons chili powder
  - 1 teaspoon cumin
  - 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  - 8 small corn tortillas
  - 1/2 cup shredded red cabbage
  - 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  - 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  - Juice of 1 lime
  - Sea salt and pepper
steps:
  - Toss cauliflower, onion, and zucchini with olive oil, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Roast at 425°F (220°C) for 15 minutes.
  - Warm tortillas and black beans on the stove.
  - Stir lime juice and pinch of salt into yogurt to create a quick drizzle.
  - Assemble tacos with beans, roasted vegetables, cabbage, cilantro, and yogurt sauce.
tags:
  - vegetarian
  - quick
  - family-friendly
---

These tacos invite customization. Add pickled onions, sliced avocado, or a dash of hot sauce.
