---
title: "Sunrise Overnight Oats"
description: "Creamy overnight oats layered with citrus zest, chia seeds, and crunchy toppings."
coverImage: "/images/recipe-overnight-oats.svg"
servings: 2
prepTime: 10
cookTime: 0
macros:
  kcal: 410
  protein: 20
  carbs: 52
  fat: 14
ingredients:
  - 1 cup rolled oats
  - 1 1/2 cups unsweetened almond milk
  - 2 tablespoons chia seeds
  - Zest of 1 orange
  - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  - 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  - 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  - 1/2 cup sliced strawberries
  - 1/4 cup toasted almonds
  - Pinch of sea salt
steps:
  - Stir oats, almond milk, chia seeds, orange zest, vanilla, honey, and salt in a jar. Chill at least 4 hours or overnight.
  - In the morning, layer oats with Greek yogurt, strawberries, and almonds. Add a splash of milk if you prefer a looser texture.
  - Pack in jars for grab-and-go breakfasts.
