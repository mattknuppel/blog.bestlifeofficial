---
title: "Rainbow Power Bowl"
description: "A high-color, high-fiber bowl layered with quinoa, roasted vegetables, and tangy citrus dressing."
coverImage: "/images/recipe-power-bowl.svg"
servings: 4
prepTime: 20
cookTime: 25
macros:
  kcal: 520
  protein: 22
  carbs: 68
  fat: 18
ingredients:
  - 1 cup dry tri-color quinoa, rinsed
  - 2 cups low-sodium vegetable broth
  - 1 large sweet potato, cubed
  - 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  - 1 cup purple cabbage, shredded
  - 2 cups baby kale
  - 1 avocado, sliced
  - 1/4 cup toasted pumpkin seeds
  - Juice of 1 orange
  - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  - 1 tablespoon tahini
  - 1 teaspoon maple syrup
  - Sea salt and cracked pepper to taste
steps:
  - Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C). Toss sweet potato and bell pepper with 1 tablespoon olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 20–25 minutes until caramelized.
  - Simmer quinoa in vegetable broth until tender, about 15 minutes. Fluff with a fork and keep warm.
  - Whisk orange juice, remaining olive oil, tahini, maple syrup, salt, and pepper into a creamy dressing.
  - Assemble bowls with quinoa, roasted vegetables, cabbage, kale, avocado, pumpkin seeds, and drizzle with dressing.
tags:
  - vegetarian
  - high-fiber
  - meal-prep
---

This rainbow bowl thrives on contrast—crispy, creamy, warm, and cool all in one bite. Roast extra vegetables and store
components separately for quick lunches.
