ingredients:
  - "2 cans (800 g) chickpeas, drained and rinsed"
  - "2 tbsp olive oil"
  - "2 tsp smoked paprika"
  - "1 tsp garlic powder"
  - "1/2 tsp cumin"
  - "1/2 tsp sea salt"
  - "1/4 tsp chilli flakes"
  - "1/2 cup roasted almonds"
  - "1/2 cup pumpkin seeds"
instructions:
  - "Pat chickpeas dry and toss with oil and spices."
  - "Roast at 200°C for 30–35 minutes, shaking tray halfway."
  - "Cool completely, then mix with almonds and pumpkin seeds."

<Callout variant="info" title="Storage">
Store in an airtight jar for up to five days. Re-crisp in the oven for a few minutes if needed.
</Callout>

## Make it sweeter

Stir in a handful of dark chocolate chips after cooling for a trail mix vibe.
