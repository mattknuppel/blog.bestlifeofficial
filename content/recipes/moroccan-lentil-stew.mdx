---
title: "Moroccan Lentil Stew"
description: "A cozy plant-based stew with smoky spices, tender lentils, and leafy greens."
coverImage: "/images/recipe-lentil-soup.svg"
servings: 6
prepTime: 15
cookTime: 35
macros:
  kcal: 360
  protein: 18
  carbs: 48
  fat: 10
ingredients:
  - 1 tablespoon olive oil
  - 1 yellow onion, diced
  - 3 cloves garlic, minced
  - 2 carrots, diced
  - 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  - 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  - 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  - 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  - 1 cup dry green lentils, rinsed
  - 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  - 4 cups low-sodium vegetable broth
  - 2 cups chopped kale
  - Juice of 1/2 lemon
  - Sea salt and pepper
steps:
  - Warm oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat. Saut√© onion, garlic, and carrots until soft.
  - Stir in spices and toast for 1 minute until fragrant.
  - Add lentils, tomatoes, broth, salt, and pepper. Simmer partially covered for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  - Stir in kale and cook 5 minutes until wilted. Finish with lemon juice.
tags:
  - vegan
  - freezer-friendly
  - high-fiber
---

Serve with whole-grain flatbread or spoon over brown rice for a heartier meal.
