---
title: "Vanilla Protein Pancakes"
description: "Fluffy pancakes boosted with protein powder and topped with berry compote."
coverImage: "/images/recipe-protein-pancakes.svg"
servings: 3
prepTime: 10
cookTime: 15
macros:
  kcal: 390
  protein: 27
  carbs: 45
  fat: 10
ingredients:
  - 1 cup oat flour
  - 1/2 cup vanilla protein powder
  - 1 teaspoon baking powder
  - 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  - 2 eggs
  - 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  - 1 cup mixed berries, frozen or fresh
  - 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup
  - Greek yogurt for serving (optional)
steps:
  - Whisk oat flour, protein powder, baking powder, and cinnamon in a bowl.
  - Add eggs, almond milk, and vanilla; stir until just combined.
  - Heat a skillet over medium heat. Pour 1/3 cup batter per pancake; cook 2â€“3 minutes per side.
  - Simmer berries with honey for 3 minutes to create compote. Serve pancakes topped with berries and yogurt.
tags:
  - breakfast
  - high-protein
  - gluten-free
---

These pancakes freeze well. Reheat in the toaster and top with extra berries for busy mornings.
